Jaeho Shin

I am a Ph.D. student in SSI lab and Computational Autonomy and Robotics Laboratory of Michigan Robotics, co-advised by Prof. Yulun Tian, and Prof. Maani Ghaffari. Prior to my Ph.D. program, I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degree in SNU, advised by Prof. Ayoung Kim of RPM Robotics Lab. My research interets are SLAM, manifold and optimization. I am particularly working on scalable scene representation using geometric primitives and relevant downstream algorithms.
